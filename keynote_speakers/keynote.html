Keynote Speakers

Dr Sam Goundar

Sam Goundar is an International Academic having taught at 13 different universities in 11 different countries with over 30 plus years of teaching and research experience in universities and tertiary institutions. At the moment, he is also a Visiting Professor of Data Science at SRM University, Chennai, India. He is a former Adjunct Professor of Information Technology (2021-2023) at The University of Fiji, Lautoka, Fiji and a former Affiliate Professor of Information Technology (2016-2020) at Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú, Lima, Peru. He was a Senior Lecturer in ICT at British University Vietnam, Senior Lecturer in IS at The University of the South Pacific, Suva, Fiji, Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, and University of Waikato, New Zealand. He was a Research Fellow at the United Nations University. He was invited as a Visiting Professor and Researcher at Hassan 1st University, Morocco (June-July 2019) and Bahir Dar University, Ethiopia (June-July 2018).

Madeshwaran Sekkarapatti Ramasamy

I have more than 12 years of research experience in developing smart polymer composite materials based on carbon materials (graphene, carbon nanotubes) and 2D materials (transition metal dichalcogenides, boron nitride) for applications in sustainable energy, sensors/actuators and as well as biomedical field. My research specifically focuses on the following topics with the aim to apply in sustainable development goals: Functionalization and dispersion chemistry of carbon nanotubes and 2D materials (graphene, molybdenum disulfide, boron nitride, vermiculite) for polymer composites applications. Developing smart 3D polymer composite structures using 3D printing and electrospinning techniques for intelligent tissue scaffolds and piezoelectric energy harvesting. Constructing solution-processed 2D materials based thin-film optoelectronic devices (polymer solar cells, dye-sensitized solar cells) for sustainable development. Structure-property relationships in polymer composites.
